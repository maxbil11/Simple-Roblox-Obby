# Simple-Roblox-Obby
The process how to create obby parts for an obby and learn some basic stuff in Roblox Studio






# Make sure to have explorer and properties open, it will make your life easier!
To insert a *script* into a part go to Explorer-[Part]-hover with your mouse over the part and click + next to it-find and insert a script (NOT A LOCAL SCRIPT!!)





#ALSO DONT FORGET TO ANCHOR THE PART, to do that, click on your part, and in properties, find a tab where it says "Anchored" click on the box, once it turns blue, your good to go!


And Follow the commands in the scripts!
