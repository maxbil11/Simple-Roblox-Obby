# Simple-Roblox-Obby
The process how to create obby parts for an obby and teach some basic stuff in Roblox Studio
# Make sure to have explorer and properties open, it will make your life easier!
To insert a *script* into a part go to Explorer-[Part]-hover with your mouse over the part and click + next to it-find and insert a script (NOT A LOCAL SCRIPT!!)


And Follow the commands in the scripts!
